Mind Tools

The youth association “Mind Tools” was founded at the end of 2017. Our main goal is to offer support to youngsters who are in the process of self-knowledge and exploring career opportunities.

We aspire for the today’s youth to become adults who will be aware of their abilities, the responsibilities that are surrounding them and the opportunities that they can benefit from. By that we aim to contribute to a more proactive and productive society in the future.

Mind Tools covers the need of the youth to learn by continuously experimenting and active living in the society.

Nowadays, the world is constantly changing and the youngsters are not systematically prepared to keep up with the innovation. The reality of this fact motivated us to contribute in offering new, useful and innovative perspectives to Romania’s youngsters.
